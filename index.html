CCTV Crime Tracker

CCTV Crime Tracker is a web application that was created with the inspiration to helping people who are affected by criminal acitivites and giving other's the opportunity to step in and help those in need. This application is used by inputting the coordinate location, the date the crime occured, and any helpful information that can be used to identify the suspect, and that information will update CCTV cameras within the radius and notify others in the area.
